## Identity System

- Display/wordmark: `Instrument Serif`
- Interface/support text: `Space Grotesk`
- Technical/callout text: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Usage Rules

- Keep minimum clear-space of `0.5x` around mark and lockups.
- Do not stretch, skew, recolor, or separate lockup components.
- Keep user/site identity primary and Praetorius attribution secondary.
- Use contrast-safe text pairings that meet WCAG 2.1 AA.
- Prefer canonical footer order: owner identity, owner links, powered-by lockup, official npm/GitHub callouts.
