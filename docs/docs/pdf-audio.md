# PDF & Audio (Page-Follow)

Praetorius syncs playback and the printed score. For demos we use a **time-sliced heuristic** (evenly divides duration across pages). For production, provide a **page map** generated by your workflow.

- Recommended: export the score to PDF at consistent DPI; avoid oversized pages.
- Audio: provide a compressed preview + optional full-res alternate.

**Deep-link format:** `?page=3&time=1:42`  
- `page` is 1-based.
- `time` is `m:ss` or seconds.
